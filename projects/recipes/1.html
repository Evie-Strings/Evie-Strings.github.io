Equipment Substitutions

To make this recipe effectivly, I recommend having a gnocchi maker to make forming the gnocchi's shape easier. If you don't have a gnocchi maker than you can roll out your dough into long strings and cut it into pieces that are about an inch and a half long. Take your thumb and press it into one side of the piece of dough. Roll your thumb away from you over the piece of dough on top of a cut glass dish so that the dough rolls around your thumb. Repeat this step for all the pieces of dough.
