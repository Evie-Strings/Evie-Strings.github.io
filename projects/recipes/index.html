Viola Family Recipes

Origins of These Recipes

These recipes were both passed down on my dad's side of the family. The recipes come from my great-great-grandmother Noni. That wasn't her real name but that's how we all know her. Noni was one of the first Italian immigrants in my family and had many memorable recipes that she passed down to her granchildren. There are far too many good family recipes that come from her and my great-grandmother Mommom to include here, so I have chosen two of my favorites.
